Crepes

Stacked crepes on a plate

Crepes are a delicious and easy breakfast option, originating in France. They can be eaten with either savory or sweet toppings and they can be easily preserved in the freezer.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs, milk, water and salt together.
  2. Gradually whisk in the flour and melted butter.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot pan.
  4. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides.
