Vanilla Pudding

Vanilla Pudding with sauce on it.

Vanilla pudding is a quick and easy desert that originated in Medieval Europe as a twist on an Arab dish. It combines simple and few ingredients into a delicious and comforting dessert.

Ingredients

  • heavy cream 35%
  • whole milk
  • sugar
  • vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • corn starch
  1. In a pot add the heavy cream, the milk, the sugar, the vanilla extract and transfer over medium heat.
  2. Let it come to a boil.
  3. In a bowl, add the egg and mix well with a hand whisk.
  4. Transfer the pot's mixture slowly into the bowl by constantly mixing.
