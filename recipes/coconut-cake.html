Coconut Cake

Fluffy coconut cake with frosting on top.

A coconut cake is an easy, quick and delicious option for a dessert on any occasion. It strikes a balance between something sweet, fresh and light. It contains few ingredients that can be easily found on your local store.

Ingredients

  • white cake mix
  • eggs
  • water
  • vegetable oil
  • milk
  • milk of coconut
  • coconut flakes
  • butter
  • powdered sugar

Steps

  1. In a bowl stir together the cake mix, eggs, water and vegetable oil until combined.
  2. Pour the butter into a baking dish.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes until the top of the cake is lightly browned.
  4. Poke holes all over the cake while it is till warm and pour mixed cream of coconut with milk.
